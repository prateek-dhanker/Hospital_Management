Hospital Guidelines

Ensure a safe and healthy environment for all patients and staff.

Guidelines for Visitors

  • Visitors must check-in at the reception.
  • Limit the number of visitors per patient.
  • Follow hygiene practices and sanitize hands regularly.
  • Respect patient privacy and maintain a quiet environment.

Guidelines for Patients

  • Adhere to the prescribed medication and treatment plan.
  • Inform the nursing staff of any changes in your condition.
  • Follow dietary restrictions if prescribed by the doctor.
  • Cooperate with hospital staff for a smooth healthcare experience.
